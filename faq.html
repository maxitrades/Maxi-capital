Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Maxi Capital challenges and funding.

What is Maxi Capital?

Maxi Capital is a prop trading evaluation platform designed to help skilled traders access funded accounts.

How does the challenge work?

Traders purchase a challenge account, follow the trading rules, and aim to hit the required profit target without violating drawdown limits.

What happens after I pass?

Traders who successfully complete the evaluation phase become eligible for funded trading accounts and profit splits.

What is the maximum drawdown?

The maximum overall drawdown is 10%, while the maximum daily loss limit is 5%.

Can I hold trades overnight?

Yes. Swing trading and overnight holding are allowed unless stated otherwise in specific challenge rules.

What markets can I trade?

Traders can trade forex pairs, gold, indices, and selected instruments depending on account type.

How are payouts handled?

Funded traders receive a percentage of profits based on the selected profit split model.

Is copy trading allowed?

Unauthorized account sharing or risky copy trading practices are prohibited to maintain fair evaluation standards.
