How It Works

Understand the full process of becoming a funded trader at Maxi Capital.

Step 1: Choose a Plan

Select a challenge account that fits your trading style. Each plan comes with a specific account size, rules, and profit target.

Step 2: Pass the Challenge

Trade responsibly and follow risk rules. Your goal is to reach the profit target without exceeding drawdown limits.

Step 3: Get Funded

Once you pass the evaluation, you receive a funded account and start earning real profit splits.

Full Trading Process

  • ✔ Register and choose your challenge plan
  • ✔ Receive your trading account credentials
  • ✔ Trade within risk management rules
  • ✔ Hit profit target (Phase 1 & Phase 2 if applicable)
  • ✔ Account verification by system/admin
  • ✔ Get funded account approval
  • ✔ Start receiving profit splits
